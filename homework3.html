<p><h2>Website Page Title</h2></p>

<p>In the web page markup, the < title > is within the < head > tag. Good page titles are particularly important for orientation, they help people know where they are and move between pages open in their browser tab. The first thing screen readers say when the user goes to a different web page is the page title.</p>
